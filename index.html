Mubarak Ahmad Rabbani Al Qadri Al Hanafi is a distinguished Islamic Research Scholar of Ahl-e-Sunnat Wal Jamaat, deeply rooted in the traditions of Sufism and correct Islamic belief (Aetiqad). Born and raised in the blessed region of Kashmir, he has dedicated his entire life to the dissemination of authentic Islamic knowledge.

A prolific author with over 15 books to his name — 12 of which have been formally published — his writings span the domains of Aqeedah, Tasawwuf, Seerat, and Islamic Research. His eloquent oratory has touched thousands of hearts across Urdu and Kashmiri-speaking communities through more than 1,000 public lectures.
